FolioSenseAI turns holdings, live market data, risk signals, and news into plain-English
portfolio context — running entirely on your machine, with Claude as an optional layer
on top, never a requirement.

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Local-first" icon="approve-check">
		Your portfolio database and config stay on your machine. Nothing ships anywhere without
		your Anthropic key.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Claude optional" icon="rocket">
		Local Intelligence runs the dashboard by default. Claude adds narration, action plans,
		and news themes on top — it never gates the core experience.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Meet Senpai" icon="puzzle">
		The dashboard's resident orb watches your portfolio and reacts to what mode it's running

	</Card>
	<Card title="Built to be read" icon="open-book">
