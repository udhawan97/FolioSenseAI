Every dashboard needs one thing staring back at you while your net worth does whatever
it's doing today. That's **Senpai** — the small orbiting mark that lives in the corner
of the dashboard, floating there whether you're checking in for the first time today or
the fifteenth.

## Senpai has moods

Senpai's commentary changes depending on what's actually running underneath it:

- **Claude connected** — sharp, a little smug, and not shy about narrating exactly what
  your portfolio is doing
- **Local Intelligence only** — dry, matter-of-fact, entirely unbothered by the absence
  of an API key
- **Claude offline** — quietly sympathetic, acknowledging the outage without making it
  weird

## Interacting with Senpai

- Tap the speech bubble to cycle to a new line on demand
- Leave it alone and it'll cycle on its own every so often
- Hide it from the overflow menu's Intelligence section if you'd rather check your
  portfolio in peace

## What Senpai is not

Senpai doesn't manage your portfolio, doesn't place trades, and isn't a substitute for
the verdict engine or action plan. Senpai has *opinions* about your portfolio. Whether
those opinions are useful is between you and Senpai.
