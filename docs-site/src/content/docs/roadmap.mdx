FolioSenseAI already earns its spot in your dock — but this is the opening chapter, and
the lab is very much open. Below is what's *on the radar* for future releases. Read it as a
peek through the workshop window, not a signed contract: every idea here is grounded in what
the app can already do, but nothing is promised on a date.

## On the radar

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="Target weights & drift" icon="random">
		Set the mix you're aiming for, then see at a glance how far today's prices have
		nudged you off plan — the same allocation and exposure math the dashboard already
		runs, pointed at your own targets.
		<br /><br />**Status:** next up in the lab · *effort: medium*
	</Card>
	<Card title="More than one portfolio" icon="bars">
		Give your retirement account and your fun-money account their own separate
		scoreboards, each with its own verdicts and analytics.
		<br /><br />**Status:** on the radar · *effort: medium*
	</Card>
	<Card title="A verdict report card" icon="star">
		The dashboard grading its own past Hold / Add / Trim calls over time — honest
		self-assessment, in public.
		<br /><br />**Status:** on the radar · *effort: small–medium*
	</Card>
	<Card title="Year-end realized recap" icon="pencil">
		A tidy "what did I actually lock in this year?" summary of realized gains, for when
		tax season comes knocking.
		<br /><br />**Status:** next up in the lab · *effort: small*
	</Card>
	<Card title="Income & dividends view" icon="analytics">
		A clear read on what your portfolio pays *you* back — the income side of holding,
		not just the price side.
		<br /><br />**Status:** being explored · *effort: medium*
	</Card>
	<Card title="A what-if simulator" icon="random">
		Preview a buy or a trim *before* you touch anything real — regret-free rehearsals
		that reuse the same exposure and risk math the dashboard already runs.
		<br /><br />**Status:** being explored · *effort: medium*
	</Card>
</CardGrid>

## What the status labels mean

These are honest confidence levels, not marketing:

| Label | Roughly means |
| --- | --- |
| **Quietly brewing** | Groundwork already exists in the app; mostly a matter of surfacing it. |
| **Next up in the lab** | Clear, self-contained, and high on the list. |
| **On the radar** | Genuinely wanted, a bit more design work before it lands. |
| **Being explored** | Promising and feasible, still being shaped. |

<SenpaiTip>
	This roadmap is less of a blood oath and more of a friendly sneak peek. FolioSenseAI is
	built part-time, so priorities may shift and you'll notice a distinct lack of hard dates.
	That's on purpose — the direction is clear (more useful, more polished, more delightful),
	the calendar is not.
</SenpaiTip>
