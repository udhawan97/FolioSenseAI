Most portfolio trackers stop at the number. Green means good, red means bad, and if you
want to know *why*, that's a separate tab, a separate app, or a group chat with someone
who read the news this morning and you didn't.

FolioSenseAI closes that gap. It's a self-hosted dashboard that combines:

- **Live portfolio math** — value, cost basis, daily change, unrealized gain, allocation
- **A verdict engine** — Hold / Add / Trim / Exit signals with confidence and scenario context
- **Analytics** — performance, risk, exposure, regime, signals, benchmark comparison
- **News** — grouped headlines for everything you hold or watch
- **Optional Claude narration** — action plans, portfolio briefings, and news themes when you bring a key

It runs on your computer, keeps its database in local SQLite, and does not connect to a
brokerage or place trades. It's for understanding what your portfolio is doing and why —
not for pulling the trigger on anything.
